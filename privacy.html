ShortsCast — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

ShortsCast is a personal content distribution tool that helps creators upload short-form videos to social media platforms including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

ShortsCast accesses the following information only with your explicit consent:

Account authentication: When you sign in with YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram, we receive an authentication token that allows the app to upload videos on your behalf. We do not store your password.

Video content: Videos you select from your device library are sent directly to the respective platform (YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram). Videos are not stored on any intermediate server.

Metadata: Titles, descriptions, and hashtags you enter are sent to the respective platform along with your video. This data is not stored separately by ShortsCast.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to upload videos to the platforms you select. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device only. No personal data is stored on external servers. All video uploads go directly from your device to the target platform.

Third-Party Services

ShortsCast integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

TikTok — Privacy Policy

Your Rights

You can revoke ShortsCast's access to any platform at any time by removing the app's permissions in your account settings on that platform. You can also sign out within the app to remove stored tokens from your device.

Children's Privacy

ShortsCast is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
